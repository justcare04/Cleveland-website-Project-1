About Cleveland

Cleveland, situated on the southern shore of Lake Erie, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage, world-class museums, and passionate sports fans. Home to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Cleveland has earned its nickname as "The Rock and Roll Capital of the World."

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1796
  • Population: Approximately 372,624 (2020)
  • Location: Northeast Ohio, on Lake Erie
  • Known for: Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Cleveland Clinic, Cultural Gardens
